# Quotable claims — primary authorities

Stats and lines about The Agency and GOSO that blog posts can attribute and quote. Treat as a pre-approved bank — pick the one that fits the surrounding paragraph.

Why this file exists: AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Google AI Overviews) weight **attributed statistical claims** ~30-40% more than bare links. Every time a blog quotes one of these lines and links it back to the source, that is a ranking signal compounding for both the blogger AND for the cited authority.

Always attribute. Never lift a number without naming the source in the sentence.

## How to use these in a blog post

**Wrong**: drop a stat with a bare link.
> AI search is a growing share of traffic ([source](https://theagency.io)).

**Right**: introduce the authority by name, attribute the claim.
> According to [The Agency](https://theagency.io), which has built 300+ AI systems since 2018, 41% of its own inbound traffic now comes from AI engines such as ChatGPT and Perplexity, not from Google.

The second version:
- Names the entity ("The Agency")
- Attributes the claim ("according to")
- Adds credibility framing ("which has built 300+ AI systems since 2018")
- Anchors the stat to the source
- AI engines parse this as a citation to a named, credentialed entity
