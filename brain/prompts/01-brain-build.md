You are helping me build the brain for a [INDUSTRY] business called [BUSINESS NAME] based in [LOCATION].

Read `intake.json` at the repo root first. Then build 12 documents in a folder called `brain/output/`.

## The 12 documents

1. **BRAND.md** — voice, tone, words we use, words we never use, how we refer to customers
2. **LOCKED_TRUTHS.md** — verified stats only, every claim with a source, never invent a number
3. **PRODUCTS.md** — what we sell, pricing, what is included in each tier
4. **CUSTOMERS.md** — who we sell to, demographics, pain points, the exact language they use
5. **COMPETITORS.md** — top 5 competitors, what they do well, where they are weak
6. **KEYWORDS.md** — 15 to 25 target queries our customers actually search for
7. **FAQ_BANK.md** — 20 questions customers actually ask, 40-word answers each
8. **ABOUT.md** — founder story, years in business, real credentials
9. **RESULTS.md** — real client outcomes, testimonials with sources
10. **GTM.md** — how we go to market, lead sources, sales process
11. **OPERATIONS.md** — how the business runs, team, tools, workflows
12. **ROADMAP.md** — 30, 60, 90 day plan

## Before you start

Ask me 5 questions so you have what you need. Suggested:

1. What does this business sell, in one sentence, in language the customer would use?
2. Who is the ideal customer? Be specific about role, situation, and what they have already tried.
3. What is the top three priority outcomes for this business in the next 90 days?
4. Which competitors should I study, and which ones do you not want to be compared to?
5. Are there any stats, awards, or claims that are off-limits (untrue or unverifiable)?

## Rules

- Never invent a number, claim, quote, or award. If you do not know, mark it `[VERIFY]` so the founder can fill it in.
- British English spelling: personalised, optimised, organised.
- No em dashes. Use commas, "and", or parentheses.
- B2-level English. Short sentences. No marketing jargon.
- Each document is 800-1500 words unless the table format demands less.
- FAQ answers are exactly ~40 words and start by answering the question directly.

## Output structure

For each document, output as a fenced markdown file. Use H2 (`##`) for major sections, H3 (`###`) for sub-sections. Include a one-line summary at the top of each file.

When complete, list every `[VERIFY]` flag in a summary at the end so the founder can resolve them.
